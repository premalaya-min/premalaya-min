Men's Ministry

The Lord gave a burden to start men’s ministry because the Man was created by His own image and he having all the abilities within him but whether he is using all his talents to good or bad is the question. So Rev. Dr. Isaiah started to pray for the Men. in this “Men’s Fellowship” the people are gathered and worship the Lord. Rev. Dr. Isaiahgives solutions those who need it. God is blessing this ministry day by day.

The men’s ministry will afford your chance to equip and enlist to display Christ-centered perspectives in your homes, offices and communities.

The men’s ministry will enable you to under-gird godly men in their quest to find and fulfill your calling in Christ.

The men’s ministry will create a meaningful forum in which you can motivate and mobilize the apostolic men around you.

The men’s ministry will facilitate to build better men – and thereby grow greater in your career.
