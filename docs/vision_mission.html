Vision & Mission

Guided by faith, compassion, and service, Premalaya Ministries continues its mission to reach the unreached and bring hope to communities through the love of Christ.

Vision

Apostle Paul wrote to the Colossians Church. We preach and teach that we present everybody perfect in Lord Jesus Christ, Colossians – 1:28. According to this verse, Premalaya Ministries was established in the year 2005 with a great vision.

To spread the Gospel to all mankind through HIS Love and Compassion.

Rev. Dr. A. Isaiah believes that the “Ministry takes place when divine resources meet human needs through loving channels to the Glory of the Lord.” It began with a burden to reach the unreached through Church Planting.

Mission

“HIS LAST COMMAND IS OUR FIRST CONCERN”

Our mission is rooted in the command given in Matthew 28:19.

Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit. The world cannot heal sorrow or turn it into joy apart from Jesus Christ.

The mission of Premalaya Ministries is to heal the brokenhearted, turn sorrow into joy, teach people to know the Love of God, and draw them to Jesus Christ.

Premalaya Ministries

Premalaya Social Development Society (PSDS), meaning "Temple of Love," is a registered non-profit organization founded in 1994 by Rev. Dr. A. Isaiah. Premalaya works with compassion and commitment to uplift the rural and urban poor, marginalized communities, scavengers, women, children, persons with disabilities, and other vulnerable sections of society.
