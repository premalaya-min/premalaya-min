Vision & Mission

Vision:

Apostle Paul wrote to the Colossians Church. We preach and teach that we present everybody perfect in Lord Jesus Christ, Colossians – 1:28. According to this verse Premalaya Ministries established in the year 2005 with a great vision.

To spread the Gospel to all mankind through HIS Love and Compassion

Rev. Dr. A. ISAIAH believes that, the “Ministry takes place when divine resources meet the human needs through loving Channels to the Glory of Lord.” It is started to reach the unreached through Church Planting.

Mission:

“HIS LAST COMMAND IS OUR FIRST CONCERN“

Our Mission is exactly what Mathew the tax collector wrote in chapter 28 verse 19.

Therefore go and make disciples of all Nations, Baptizing them in the name of Father and of the Son and the Holy Spirit. The world cannot heal and turn the sorrow into joy except Jesus Christ

The mission of Premalaya ministries is “To heal the broken Hearted and turn their sorrows into joy, teach the people to know the Love of God and drove them to Jesus Christ.”

Premalaya Ministries

Premalaya Social Development Society (PSDS), meaning "Temple of Love," is a registered non-profit organization founded in 1994 by Rev. Dr. A. Isaiah. Premalaya works with compassion and commitment to uplift the rural and urban poor, marginalized communities, scavengers, women, children, persons with disabilities, and other vulnerable sections of society.
