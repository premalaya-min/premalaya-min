Youth Ministry

Great burden that God has given to Rev. Dr. Isaiah focuses on youth ministry; this ministry is called as “Youth Blessing Ministry”. Nowadays young people need Jesus Christ in their lives as much as anyone else because Satan has strategically focused his warfare on youths. That’s why Rev. Isaiah with a great burden conducting and performing theyouth meetings to reach the unreached youth and transforming their lives to Christ Jesus. Through hisyouth meetings Rev. Isaiah guiding the youth in various aspects like, how to walk with God, How to focus our Lives, How to stand firm, How to break the barricades of addictions, how to filled with the Holy Spirit, how to reach out to one another with love and compassion, and share the gospel message.

Through “Youth Blessing Ministry” young people are reaching out to their unreached friends and inviting them to participate in this meetings. Once the young people are touched by the Lord they are becoming more active and involving in their church ministries. Not only involving in church ministry but they also ministering to each other in offices, collages and even in schools and that is the essence of youth ministry what God gave to Rev. Isaiah. In Every concert thousands of youth are participating and receiving the blessing from the Lord.


In this Youth Blessing ministry

Rev. Dr. Isaiah focusing the below points

  • A safe, loving, and wholesome atmosphere for young people
  • A program which helps young people become formed in their faith
  • A variety of worship experiences that help young people connect with God and one another
  • Teaching about the how to spend time with God, how to raise our hands to help others to overcome their struggles, God’s Love, how to build their career, how to develop their individual prayer and spiritual lifeand become successful champion. Assisting theyouth in developing values, ethical norms, and a sense of Christian community, interaction with adults who authentically model the Christian faith. Rev. Isaiah himself involve in performing some activities like how to worship,how to preach the Gospel to unreached all these are involved fun and the building of sense of worth to the youth.
