Youth Ministry

Great burden that God has given to Rev. Dr. Isaiah focuses on youth ministry; this ministry is called as “Youth Blessing Ministry”. Nowadays young people need Jesus Christ in their lives as much as anyone else because Satan has strategically focused his warfare on youths. That’s why Rev. Isaiah with a great burden conducts youth meetings to reach the unreached youth and transform their lives to Christ Jesus. Through his youth meetings Rev. Isaiah guides the youth in various aspects like how to walk with God, how to focus our lives, how to stand firm, how to break the barricades of addictions, how to be filled with the Holy Spirit, how to reach out to one another with love and compassion, and share the gospel message.

Through “Youth Blessing Ministry” young people are reaching out to their unreached friends and inviting them to participate in these meetings. Once the young people are touched by the Lord they are becoming more active and involved in their church ministries. Not only involving in church ministry but they also minister to each other in offices, colleges and even in schools and that is the essence of youth ministry God gave to Rev. Isaiah. In every concert thousands of youth are participating and receiving the blessing from the Lord.

In this Youth Blessing Ministry

Rev. Dr. Isaiah focuses on the following points:

  • A safe, loving, and wholesome atmosphere for young people
  • A program which helps young people become formed in their faith
  • A variety of worship experiences that help young people connect with God and one another
  • Teaching about how to spend time with God, how to raise our hands to help others overcome their struggles, God’s Love, how to build their career, how to develop their individual prayer and spiritual life and become successful champions. Assisting the youth in developing values, ethical norms, and a sense of Christian community, interaction with adults who authentically model the Christian faith. Rev. Isaiah himself is involved in performing some activities like how to worship, how to preach the Gospel to the unreached—all these are involved fun and the building of sense of worth to the youth.
