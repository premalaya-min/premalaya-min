Prayer Ministry

The Bible speaks much about prayer. But, sometimes, too often, we ignore prayer and seek to accomplish in the strength of our own wills those things that we desire to have or happen.

Premalaya Ministries personally prays for those who require prayer help through their prayer cells. The following activities are prime focus of Premalaya Ministries:

  • Personal Prayers by Rev. Dr. Isaiah
  • Personal Counseling
  • Hospital visit and offer prayers for patient
  • Prison Visit and offer Prayer for the prisoners
  • Prayer through telephone
  • Prayer Conferences
  • Cottage Prayer Cells
  • Fasting prayers
  • Promise Verse for the Day (Promise verse with small Prayer)
